Focus Flow

Cross-Platform Productivity App Built with Flutter & Dart

cd ../projects

The Problem

Students frequently experience academic burnout due to disorganized coursework and inefficient study habits. Managing multiple syllabi manually leads to missed deadlines, and generic, one-size-fits-all study guides fail to address individual learning gaps.

The Proposed Solution

I am currently developing FocusFlow, a cross-platform student productivity hub built with Flutter and Dart. The platform is being engineered for an initial launch on Web and Android, utilizing a scalable architecture that allows for a seamless future deployment to iOS. It is designed to unify task management and scheduling into one centralized experience.

Key Architecture & Features:

  • Smart Task Management: A dynamic, state-managed To-Do list for daily academic tracking.
  • AI-Powered Timetables: Users will be able to manually input their schedules or upload a PDF syllabus, utilizing AI parsing to automatically extract and populate their weekly timetable.
  • Universal Calendar Sync: Integrating with native APIs to establish a two-way sync with Google Calendar and Apple Calendar, ensuring all events live in one unified dashboard.
